About MP Drafting

Precision Planning for Commercial Glazing

We are a drafting firm that specializes in commercial glazing. Not a general CAD service. Not an engineering substitute. A coordination specialist — built around the discipline that makes glass projects work.

Our Position

Drafting Is a Professional Planning Discipline — Not an Afterthought

The perception that shop drawings are something you "knock out" after engineering — or that any CAD operator can produce a usable set — has cost glazing contractors real money and real credibility on real jobs.

We built MP Drafting to change that perception from the inside. Thorough drawings don't just look better. They coordinate better. Architects approve them faster. Field crews execute them cleanly. Engineers don't have to revise against them.

That's not marketing language. That's what our repeat customers come back for.

Why Repeat Customers Stay

The Emotional Driver Isn't Price

Our repeat customers aren't the cheapest option seekers. They're experienced PMs who have been burned by incomplete drawings, missed fasteners, and submittals that came back rejected. They return because:

The drawings are thorough

Every detail reflects actual field conditions. Surrounding conditions are drawn from the architectural set, not assumed or templated.

Fasteners are accurate

Fasteners are matched to the condition type. Not listed as "installer to verify." Not left as placeholders on Typical Detail jobs.

Layout dimensions are clear

Column lines, IN/OUT dimensions, and layout clarity mean field crews can execute without guessing. No field RFIs chasing missing dimensions.

Submittals are architect-ready

Clean title blocks, organized sheet sets, clarifying notes. Architects approve our submittals faster because they're presented like professional documents, not CAD exports.

Communication is responsive

One contact. Clear status. No chasing. When you need an update, you get one. When there's an issue, we flag it — we don't wait for you to find it.

The process is structured

KO form. Preview stage. Final delivery. Engineering coordination simplified into one channel. You don't babysit the drafter. That's the point.
