Our Approach

Structured from day one

When a project is in motion, PMs do not have time to manage a drafting vendor. Our workflow is designed to reduce back-and-forth and prevent delays caused by missing inputs.

  • → Preview step upfront to catch missing info and remove preventable delays
  • → Kickoff (KO) form to capture job-specific preferences and scope early
  • → Clear questions and coordination during drafting, including live review calls when needed

Engineering

Planning first. Engineering as structural validation.

MP Drafting produces coordinated shop drawings that resolve real-world conditions, fabrication detail, and field layout clarity. When engineering is required, it functions as structural validation and is coordinated in a way that keeps roles clear. We do not present drafting as a substitute for a licensed professional engineer.

  • → Shop drawings coordinate reality: dimensions, surrounding conditions, and installation clarity
  • → Engineering validates structural performance and delegated design intent (when required)
