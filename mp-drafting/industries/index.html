Industries We Serve

Commercial glazing appears across every building type. MP Drafting brings the same precision and coordination discipline to each — from office towers to municipal facilities.

The Same Standard Across Every Sector

Glazing systems vary by building type — but the standard for shop drawings doesn't. Every sector has its own code requirements, submittal expectations, and coordination complexity. MP draws to the actual conditions of your project, not a generic template.

Whether you're working on a high-rise curtain wall or a ground-floor storefront on a municipal building, the drawings will be thorough, coordinated, and architect-ready.

What stays the same regardless of building type: accurate surrounding conditions, matched fasteners, column line references, IN/OUT dimensions, clarifying notes, and architect-ready submittal format.
